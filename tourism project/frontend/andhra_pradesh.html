Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is a state in the southeastern part of India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical sites, and beautiful landscapes. The state's capital is Amaravati, and it offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity. Explore the beautiful temples, serene beaches, and vibrant festivals that make Andhra Pradesh a must-visit destination for travelers.
