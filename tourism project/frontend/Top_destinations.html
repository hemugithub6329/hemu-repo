Top Destinations

Goa

Goa is India's party capital with beautiful beaches, nightlife, and a laid-back vibe. Visit Baga Beach, Dudhsagar Waterfalls, and Fort Aguada.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its historic forts, palaces, and vibrant markets. Explore Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal.

Kerala

Kerala is known for its lush green landscapes and backwaters. Enjoy a houseboat cruise in Alleppey, explore Munnar's tea plantations, and visit Kochi.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Varanasi, one of the oldest cities in the world, is a spiritual hub on the banks of the Ganges River. Witness mesmerizing Ganga Aarti and explore ancient temples.

Leh-Ladakh

Leh-Ladakh offers stunning landscapes with high mountain ranges, serene lakes, and monasteries. Don't miss Pangong Lake and Nubra Valley.

Mumbai, Maharashtra

Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is a bustling metropolis with iconic landmarks like Gateway of India and Marine Drive. Explore Bollywood and street food.

Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Agra is famous for the stunning Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Visit Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri for more historical wonders.

Udaipur, Rajasthan

Udaipur is known as the City of Lakes and is famous for its beautiful palaces and lakes. Explore the City Palace and Lake Pichola.

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is a popular hill station with colonial architecture and scenic beauty. Visit the Mall Road and Kufri.

Kolkata, West Bengal

Kolkata is known for its rich cultural heritage, art, and literature. Explore Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, and try local sweets.
