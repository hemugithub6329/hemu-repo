  1. Sri Venkateswara Temple

    Sri Venkateswara Temple is a famous pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Venkateswara located in Tirumala. It is one of the richest and most-visited temples in the world.

  2. Kanaka Durga Temple

    Kanaka Durga Temple is a renowned temple dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga located in Vijayawada. It is perched on the Indrakeeladri hill overlooking the Krishna River.

  3. Simhachalam Temple

    Simhachalam Temple is an ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Varaha Narasimha located in Visakhapatnam. It is known for its beautiful architecture and religious significance.

  4. Lepakshi Temple

    Lepakshi Temple is a unique and historical temple located in Anantapur district known for its exquisite architecture and famous hanging pillar.

  5. Kalahasti Temple

    Kalahasti Temple, also known as Srikalahasti Temple, is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is famous for its Vayu Linga. It is located in Srikalahasti town of Chittoor district.
